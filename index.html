Controls

Things you have to know about bathyscaphe handling

Handling

  • Bathyscaphe's handling represented by four directions, each direction have 3 levels of speed.
  • To guide a ship to some side of a screen, press corresponding arrow-key on keyboard.
  • To fasten your ship in some direction, press corresponding arrow-key once again.
  • If you need to stop your ship, press arrow-key that is opposite to current direction.

Status bar

  • Status bar represents the current ship's status.
  • The health bar shows how much hit points left.

Score

  • The highscore panel shows how deep you've been at current session.

Ammo panel

  • Shows the current weapon type.
  • The bottom circle contains shots remaining for selected weapon.

Attack

  • To launch a torpedo, press Z button

Depth charge

  • Launch Depth charge if you encountered a mine below you in a narrow tunnel with X button

Pause menu

  • You can access pause menu by pressing Esc
  • Here you can turn on\off in-game sounds, unpause or restart the game
